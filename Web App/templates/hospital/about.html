ABOUT US

Opening its doors in August 2006 and situated beside the picturesque Bashundhara Dhaka, Bangladesh, Cooper hospital is one of the largest private secto healthcare facilities in Bangladesh.We are committed to (i) Quality, (i Effectiveness, (iii) Integration, (iv) Caring for the Community, (v) Caring for our staff and (vi) Research & Training.








MISSION

To build a legacy of impact driven, accessible, safe and private healthcare for patients in need.

VISION

To be the leading integrated healthcare network in emerging markets, transforming the quality of healthcare.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Cooper Hospital has pioneered in many endeavors and has achieved many breakthrough milestones.
